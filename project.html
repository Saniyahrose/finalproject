About Us

Our mission is to educate travelers on all that the bay area has to offer, starting with Wine. We offer guided tours to Napa, Ca where a lot of Vineyards are located. We are currently catering to people traveling into the Bay Area looking to get some history on what makes it so special. Each tour comes with complimentary charcuterie boards and champagne. We will also stop at scenic viewpoints.

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Saniyah Reed, and I am passionate about exploring the San Francisco Bay Area. One of my favorite activities is discovering new viewpoints that offer breathtaking panoramas of this beautiful region. I love nothing more than sharing these experiences with others and showing them the hidden gems of the Bay Area. In addition to my love for the outdoors, I also enjoy creating charcuterie boards. It's my way of expressing my creativity while bringing people together around delicious food. If you book a tour with my company, you can expect to learn a lot about the area, experience its natural beauty, and indulge in some scrumptious snacks. So join me on an adventure and let's explore the San Francisco Bay Area together!
